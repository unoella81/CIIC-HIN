CIIC-HIN is a company and serves as an NGO, birthed in Rwanda
motivated by a passion to develop and improve evidence-based
decision making in healthcare provision and policies in the region.

We aim to do this through building the capacity of health and
educational professionals with relevant research skills, while
also implementing robust research with and for our partners.
